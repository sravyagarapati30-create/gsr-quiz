Section 01

Overview

Welcome to KidZone — a fun and educational website featuring quiz games, memory games, number guessing games, and a leaderboard for kids and young people.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website, how we use it, and how we keep it safe. We take your privacy very seriously, especially because our website is designed for children and youth.

ℹ️ By using KidZone, you and your parent or guardian agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the website.

This policy applies to all visitors of our website and is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.

Section 02

Information We Collect

We aim to collect as little information as possible. Here is what may be collected when you use KidZone:

Information you provide directly:

  • Player Name / Nickname — When you enter a name or nickname to appear on the leaderboard, this is stored locally in your browser (not on our servers).
  • Game Scores — Your scores from quizzes, memory games, and number guessing games are saved locally on your device using browser storage.

Information collected automatically:

  • Device & Browser Information — Basic technical data such as browser type, device type, and screen size may be collected to help the website display correctly.
  • Usage Data — Pages visited, time spent on games, and general interaction data may be logged for website improvement purposes.
  • IP Address — Your IP address may be collected automatically by our server or by third-party services such as advertising networks.
✅ We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personally identifiable information to play our games.

Section 03

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit.

How we use cookies:

  • Essential Cookies — Used to save your game progress, scores, and player nickname locally so you can continue where you left off.
  • Analytics Cookies — We may use tools like Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This helps us make the site better. These cookies do not identify you personally.
  • Advertising Cookies — If Google AdSense ads are displayed on our website, Google may use cookies to show relevant ads. See Section 4 for more details.
💡 You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. However, disabling certain cookies may affect some features of the website, such as saving your scores.

Most browsers allow you to view, manage, and delete cookies. To learn more, visit your browser's help section or go to www.allaboutcookies.org.

Section 04

Third-Party Advertising

KidZone may display advertisements in the future using Google AdSense or similar advertising services. These ads help us keep the website free to use.

How Google AdSense works:

  • Google uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other websites on the internet.
  • We do not control the content of these ads or the data collected by Google for advertising purposes.
  • Third-party advertising partners may collect information such as IP address, device identifiers, and browsing behaviour to display relevant advertisements.
⚠️ Important for Parents: If our site is used by children under 13, we will ensure that Google AdSense is configured in non-personalized ad mode so that interest-based or behavioural advertising is not served to young children, in compliance with COPPA.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings at www.google.com/settings/ads, or by visiting www.aboutads.info to opt out of interest-based advertising by other participating companies.

Section 05

Children's Privacy

Protecting the privacy of children is very important to us. KidZone is designed as a safe, educational, and entertaining environment for kids and youth.

🛡️ We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Our commitments to children's safety:

  • We do not require children to register or create an account to use our website.
  • We do not ask children for their real name, email address, phone number, home address, or any other personal details.
  • Player nicknames entered for the leaderboard are stored only locally on the user's own device and are not sent to our servers.
  • We do not allow children to post publicly visible content or communicate with other users.
  • When running ads, we configure them to show non-personalised advertisements to children.
  • If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

For Parents and Guardians:

We encourage parents and guardians to supervise their children's online activity. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately at the details in Section 10 and we will take steps to remove that information.

Section 08

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you (or your parent/guardian on your behalf) may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Right to Access — You may request a copy of any personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Delete — You may request that we delete any personal data we have collected.
  • Right to Correct — You may ask us to correct any inaccurate information.
  • Right to Object — You may object to certain types of data processing, such as advertising.

Since KidZone stores game data (scores and nicknames) locally in your browser, you can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or cookies. To exercise any other rights, please contact us using the details in Section 10.
